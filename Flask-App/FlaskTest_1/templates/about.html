About

Olin GotMail is a way to check on your mailbox from anywhere. On the main screen, enter your mailbox number, and we'll let you know whether you've got mail!

Alternatively, use can use your mailbox's unique link to check your mail directly. To get your mailbox's unique link, append "/olin/mb[YOUR-MAILBOX-NUMBER]" or even just "/olin/[YOUR-MAILBOX-NUMBER]" to our home web address!

To learn more, please visit our website. You may also see our code hosted on GitHub. Thank you!
